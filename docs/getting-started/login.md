# Login to Classtrix

## Purpose

This page explains how staff members sign in to Classtrix. Use it at the start of the day or whenever you are signed out of the system.

## Steps

### Step 1

Open the Classtrix login page in your browser using the link shared by your institute.

### Step 2

Enter your username, email, or mobile number, depending on how your institute has set up your account.

### Step 3

Enter your password carefully and check that Caps Lock is not turned on.

### Step 4

Click the login button and wait for the dashboard to open.

## Tips

- Save the login page in your browser bookmarks for quick access.
- If you share a system at the front desk, always log out before leaving.
- Use the Forgot Password option if it is available for your account.
