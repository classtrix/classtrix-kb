# Student Registration

## Purpose

Student registration creates the full student profile after admission is confirmed. Use it to store personal details, parent information, academic details, and batch assignment.

## Steps

### Step 1

Open the Admissions module and choose the option to register a student.

### Step 2

Enter the student’s personal details, parent or guardian details, address, and school or college information if needed.

### Step 3

Select the course, session, branch, and batch in which the student will study.

### Step 4

Save the registration and note the generated student ID or registration number.

## Tips

- Keep admission forms or supporting documents nearby while entering details.
- Double-check mobile numbers because they are often used for future communication.
- Assign the correct batch before saving to avoid timetable confusion later.
