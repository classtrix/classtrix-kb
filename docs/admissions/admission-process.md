# Admission Process

## Purpose

This page explains how to move an interested lead into a confirmed admission. Use it when a student has agreed to join a course and the team is ready to complete the admission details.

## Steps

### Step 1

Open the lead record and confirm the student’s course, session, branch, and preferred batch.

### Step 2

Click the option to convert the lead into an admission.

### Step 3

Review the admission form, then enter fee plan details, discount or scholarship if approved, and joining date.

### Step 4

Save the admission so the student can be registered and linked to the correct course and batch.

## Tips

- Confirm the student name spelling before final approval.
- Check approved discounts carefully before saving the admission.
- Complete the admission on the same day to keep reports current.
