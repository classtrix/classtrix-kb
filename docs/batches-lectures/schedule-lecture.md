# Schedule a Lecture

## Purpose

Lecture scheduling helps your team plan classes for each batch. Use it to set subject, teacher, date, time, and room details so students and staff can follow the timetable.

## Steps

### Step 1

Open the batch for which you want to add a lecture.

### Step 2

Go to the lecture or timetable section and choose Add Lecture.

### Step 3

Select the subject, faculty member, date, start time, end time, and classroom or online link.

### Step 4

Save the lecture and confirm that it appears in the batch schedule.

## Tips

- Check faculty availability before finalizing the lecture.
- Add lecture topics in the notes field if your team wants better lesson tracking.
- Review the weekly timetable regularly to avoid timing conflicts.
