# View Sales Reports

## Purpose

Sales reports help management and counselors understand how enquiries, admissions, and fee collections are performing. Use them to review branch activity and support decision-making.

## Steps

### Step 1

Open the Reports module and select the sales or admission report you want to view.

### Step 2

Choose the date range, branch, counselor, course, or lead source filters as needed.

### Step 3

Click Apply or View Report to load the latest figures.

### Step 4

Review the totals and export the report if you need to share it with management.

## Tips

- Use the same date range when comparing one report with another.
- Check counselor and source filters carefully before sharing the report.
- Export reports at the end of the day or week for branch review meetings.
